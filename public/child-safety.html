Swiip Child Safety Standards

Our commitment to protecting minors and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)

Age Restriction Policy

Swiip is intended for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly allow users under the age of 18 to create accounts or use our services.

  • Users must confirm they are 18+ during registration
  • Accounts suspected of being operated by minors are immediately suspended
  • We actively monitor and remove underage users from our platform

Child Safety Measures

We implement comprehensive measures to protect minors and prevent CSAE:

  • Content Moderation: We actively monitor and remove any content that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes minors
  • Zero Tolerance Policy: Any content depicting child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is immediately removed and reported to authorities
  • User Reporting: Users can report concerning content or behavior directly within the app
  • Automated Detection: We use technology to detect and prevent the sharing of inappropriate content involving minors

Reporting Mechanisms

If you encounter any content or behavior that may involve child exploitation or abuse:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the report button on any profile or message
  • Email: Contact us at safety@swiip.app
  • Emergency: Contact local law enforcement immediately for imminent threats

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours and appropriate action is taken.

Cooperation with Authorities

Swiip cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies worldwide:

  • We report all CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • We preserve and provide data when legally required for investigations
  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws in every jurisdiction we operate
