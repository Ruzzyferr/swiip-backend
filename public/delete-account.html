Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy. If you wish to delete your Conversa account and permanently remove all your data, you can do so using one of the following methods:

📱 Method 1: In-App (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your data.

  1. Open the Conversa app.
  2. Go to your Profile tab.
  3. Tap on Settings (Gear Icon).
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your choice. Your data will be deleted immediately.

📧 Method 2: Web Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion here.

Data Retention: Once processed, all your personal data including messages, matches, and profile photos will be permanently removed from our servers.
