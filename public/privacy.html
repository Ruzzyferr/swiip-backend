Privacy Policy for Conversa

Effective Date: January 4, 2026

Conversa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Conversa.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile information (photos, interests, bio)
  • Location data (to show nearby users)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Match you with other users
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy (e.g., with your consent, for legal reasons, or with service providers like Google/Firebase for analytics).

4. Your Choices

You can update your profile information at any time through the app. You may also request account deletion via the settings menu.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@conversa.app.
